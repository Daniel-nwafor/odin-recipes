fresh cranberry

Opt for fresh cranberries that are firm, plump, and blemish-free.

lighter colored cranberries are best for sauce, as they contain more

pectin and result in a thicker consistency.

orange juice

All cranberry sauce starts with a liquid base. If you don't like orange juice,

you can substitute pomegranate juice or even red wine.

sugar

White sugar does more than add sweetness to classic cranberry sauce:

It also absorbs liquid and, when heated, thickens the sauce.
